
To get the newest google robot model and objects in your environment editor to drop in the bin, do:

- Copy Models/google_grasp to your $HBP/Models
- Copy Models/ur_description to your $HBP/Models
- Copy Models/musc_arm robot to your $HBP/Models
- Copy Models/objects folder to your $HBP/Models
- substitute $HBP/Models/libraries/model_library.json with the file in this repo
- Run $HBP/Models/create_symlinks
- Run configure_nrp
- clear your browser cache and cookies
